Material Science and Engineering

China Steel Research is committed to the construction of new material research and development bases for national economy and national defense industry, undertaking the research and development tasks of more than 85% of new materials for national defense industry in metallurgical industry in China. It has successively worked for the "two bombs and one star", "Long March series carrier rockets", "Shenzhou spacecraft", "Chang'e" lunar exploration project, new fighter planes, military electronic materials, ships, new tanks, artillery and so on.
